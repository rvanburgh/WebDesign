Dysfunctional Attitude Test

Because of the crucial importance of eliciting the silent assumptions that give rise to your mood swings, a method called the "Dysfunctional Attitude Scale" (DAS) has been developed by Dr. Arlene Weissman. She has compiled a list of one hundred self-defeating attitudes that commonly occur in individuals predisposed to emotional disorders. Her research has indicated that while negative automatic thoughts are reduced dramatically between episodes of depression, a self-defeating belief system remains more or less constant during episodes of depression and remission. Dr. Weissman's studies confirm the concept that your silent assumptions represent a predisposition to emotional turbulence that you carry with you at all times.
